# Torvionyx Beta Recruitment Playbook

**Goal:** Recruit 20–50 beta users in 1–2 weeks and get quality feedback.

**Mindset:** You're not selling. You're inviting smart people to help you build.

---

## Week 1: Prepare & Launch

### Day 1: Prepare Your Materials

**Create these NOW:**

1. **Sign-up link:** Your app's sign-up page
2. **Feedback link:** TypeForm, Google Form, or Slack channel
3. **Email template:** Copy below
4. **Elevator pitch:** A 1-sentence explanation

**Elevator pitch (pick one):**
- "AI proposal writer for freelancers — turns your brief into a beautiful proposal in 60 seconds."
- "Sick of spending hours writing proposals? Torvionyx does it for you in 1 minute."

---

## Day 1–2: Direct Outreach (Most Effective)

**Who to reach out to:**
- Freelancer friends, colleagues, past clients
- People on LinkedIn who are designers, developers, consultants
- Slack groups you're in
- People in your contact list who are self-employed
